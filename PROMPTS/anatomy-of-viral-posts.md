# Prompt: Anatomy of Viral Posts (Break Down + Rebuild)

This is Dan Koe's secret technique. Break down tweets you love → learn the hidden structure → rebuild as YOUR prompt.

---

## Step 1: Break Down Any Post You Love

```
I want you to break down this post so I can recreate it from scratch.

Break down:
1. Core structure (hook, body, payoff)
2. Why it works (psychological patterns)
3. The psychological patterns at play
4. What context is needed to execute this
5. Anything else needed to understand how to recreate it

POST TO ANALYZE:
[Paste a tweet/post you admire here]
```

---

## Step 2: Combine Into a Complete Guide

Run step 1 on 3 tweets you love, then ask:

```
Now combine all three breakdowns into a singular guide: "The Anatomy of Viral [Type] Posts — A Complete Guide"

Include:
1. The archetypes (what types of posts are these?)
2. Core psychological patterns (what makes people stop scrolling?)
3. Technical structure (how is it built sentence by sentence?)
4. Power techniques (what specific tricks make it work?)
5. What context is needed from the writer to execute well
```

---

## Step 3: Extract What the AI Needs FROM YOU

```
Based on this complete guide, what is everything you would need from me to start writing posts like this?

List:
- Core identity
- Audience psychology  
- Philosophical stance
- Voice parameters
- Specific insights I have
- Transformation narrative
- Emotional territory I operate in
- Anything else
```

---

## Step 4: Build Your Own Prompt (The Meta-Move)

Take the guide from Step 2 + the context from Step 3 → feed into a "prompt that helps you create prompts" → get a custom prompt for your voice.

```
I want to create a two-phase prompt:
- Phase 1: Context gathering — interview me for the ideas I want to write about
- Phase 2: Post writing — write three variations of each type of post using the ideas I gave you

Here is the content breakdown guide:
[Paste guide from Step 2]

Here is what context is needed from me:
[Paste context from Step 3]
```

---

## What You Get

A custom tweet-writing prompt trained on posts YOU admire, in YOUR voice, with YOUR context built in. This is the difference between asking AI "write me 10 tweets" (garbage) and giving it everything it needs to write tweets you'd actually post.

## The Full Anatomy of Viral Philosophical Posts

From Dan Koe's analysis, viral philosophical posts contain:

**Structure:**
1. Provocative thesis (the bold claim)
2. Purge list (what people are doing wrong)
3. The promise (what's possible)
4. The elite few (who gets it)
5. Permission structure (why it's okay to feel/think this)

**Psychological Patterns:**
- Paradoxes and counterintuitive truths
- Transformation arcs (before/after)
- Pattern interrupts (stops the scroll)
- Specificity over generic advice
- Vulnerability and failure admission

**Power Techniques:**
- Short sentences that hit hard
- Strategic whitespace
- No hashtags, no emojis
- Ends with a question or challenge
- First person, always
