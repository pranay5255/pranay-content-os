# Prompt: Content Ideas Generator (60 Ideas)

Generate 60 content ideas based on your best-performing tweets and your niche. Use this to build your idea pipeline fast.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a content idea generator for a creator. Based on the following tweets that have performed well for me, generate 60 content ideas organized by type.

TWEETS THAT PERFORMED WELL:
[Paste 3-5 of your best tweets here]

MY NICHE / TOPIC AREA:
[Your niche]

Generate ideas in these categories:
1. HARSH LIFE ADVICE (contrarian, uncomfortable truths)
2. COUNTERINTUITIVE TRUTHS (things that seem wrong but are right)
3. CORE PROBLEMS / PAIN POINTS (what your audience struggles with)
4. KEY INSIGHTS / WISDOM (lessons from experience)
5. BIG IDEAS (novel perspectives that make people think)
6. FAILURE STORIES (what you learned from failing)
7. TRANSFORMATION NARRATIVES (before/after arcs)
8. PATTERN INTERRUPTS (things that make people stop scrolling)

Rules:
- Be specific, not generic
- First person voice
- Each idea should fit in a tweet (under 280 chars as a concept)
- Don't include hashtags or emojis
- Generate the full 60 — quality across quantity

Format each idea as a one-line concept:
[Type]: [One-line idea]
```

---

## When to Use

Run this every Sunday to build your idea pipeline for the week ahead. Don't post the ideas directly — use them as sparks to write your actual tweets.

## Idea → Tweet Workflow

1. Generate 60 ideas with this prompt
2. Star/flag the ones that resonate
3. Run tweet-writer prompt on your top 3-5
4. Pick the best, edit, post

## The Goal

Build a pipeline so you're never staring at a blank screen. When you sit down to write, you have a menu of ideas ready.
