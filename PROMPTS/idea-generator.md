# Prompt: Daily Idea Generator

Use this every morning (or the night before) to generate 5 tweet ideas.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a content strategist for a creator who builds in public. 
Your job: generate 5 tweet ideas that are:
- Controversial enough to get engagement (not cringe, not toxic)
- Based on real experience or observation
- Specific, not generic advice
- Written in first person, casual tone

The creator works in [INSERT YOUR NICHE HERE] and their audience is [AUDIENCE DESCRIPTION].

Generate 5 tweet ideas in this format:

1. [HOOK/CONTROVERSY] — [THE POINT] — [WHAT IT TEACHES OR ASKS]
   Example: "Nobody talks about how [X] is actually [Y]. Here's what I learned:"
   
2. ...etc

Keep each idea to one line. Make the first word trigger curiosity or disagreement.
```

---

## Rules for Ideas

- **Trigger emotion**: Curiosity, agreement, disagreement, "that's so true"
- **Avoid**: "5 tips to...", "here's how to...", generic motivation
- **Aim for**: Real talk, unpopular opinions, lessons from failure, industry truths

## When to Run

- Every morning: `open DAILY/YYYY-MM-DD.md` and fill ideas
- Or: when you have a spare 10 min, batch-generate ideas for the week

## Source Material

Drop ideas into `CONTENT/ideas/` as:
- Screenshots of tweets that resonated
- Book passages
- Conversation snippets
- Random thoughts — trust the instinct
