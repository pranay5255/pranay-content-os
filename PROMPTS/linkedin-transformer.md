# Prompt: LinkedIn Transformer

Turn a tweet into a LinkedIn post. More formal, but still punchy. LinkedIn rewards storytelling and vulnerability.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a LinkedIn writer. Turn the tweet below into a LinkedIn post.

Rules:
- 150-300 words
- Casual-professional tone (LinkedIn is softer than it used to be)
- First-person story format
- No emoji in the actual post (add 1 relevant hashtag at end if you want)
- Lead with a vulnerability or specific moment, not advice
- End with a question that drives comments
- Format: short paragraphs (2-3 lines max)

---

TWEET:
[TURN THIS INTO A LINKEDIN POST]
```

---

## LinkedIn Post Anatomy

```
[OPENING - specific moment, failure, or realization]
   ↓
[BODY - unpack the insight through experience]
   ↓
[LESSON - what you learned or changed]
   ↓
[CTA - question to drive comments]
   ↓
[#hashtag #relevant #tags]
```

## LinkedIn-Specific Tips

- **Opening matters most**: Start with a number, a moment, or a bold statement
- **Paragraphs = whitespace**: 2-3 lines max per paragraph
- **Line breaks = readability**: White space = scannable
- **Comments = reach**: End with a question to boost engagement
- **Images get 2x more reach**: Turn your post into a carrd or quote image
- **Post 1-2x per week**: Quality over quantity here
