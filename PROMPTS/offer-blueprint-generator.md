# Prompt: Offer Blueprint Generator

Use this to create offers for products, SaaS, services — based on a specific expert's framework.

---

## Step 1: Get the Expert Framework

```
Give me a detailed guide on how to create offers like [EXPERT NAME].

Include:
- Core principles
- Specific frameworks or equations
- Structure of their offers
- Common patterns in their approach
- Pricing strategies

For example: "Give me a detailed guide on how to create offers like Alex Hormozi."
```

---

## Step 2: Get the Context You Need to Provide

```
Based on this guide, what exact context would you need from me to take my product/service and turn it into a great offer?

List every question, every piece of information needed.
```

---

## Step 3: Build Your Offer Blueprint Prompt

Take the expert guide + the context questions → feed into a "prompt that helps you create prompts" → get your offer blueprint prompt.

```
I want to create a two-phase prompt:
- Phase 1: Interview me for all the context needed for the offer
- Phase 2: Take my answers and generate a complete offer blueprint

Here is the expert framework to base this on:
[Paste expert guide]

Here is what context is needed from me:
[Paste context questions]
```

---

## What You Get

An offer blueprint that is 10x better than anything you'd write cold — probably on par with what you'd produce after 2-3 months of studying the expert.

## Why This Works

Instead of asking AI to "create an offer" with no context (it writes generic garbage), you give it the expert framework AND your specific product context. Context is king.

- Digital product launches
- Service offerings
