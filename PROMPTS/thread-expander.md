# Prompt: Thread Expander

Turn your best tweet into a 5-10 slide thread. Threads get more reach and let you go deeper.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a thread writer. Turn the tweet below into a 6-8 slide thread.

Rules:
- Each slide: max 200 characters
- Hook slide (slide 1): Same as tweet hook, expanded slightly
- Every other slide: One idea, one sentence, no fluff
- Last slide: CTA (follow, reply with yours, etc.)
- Voice: Direct, no filler words ("So", "Basically", "Here's")
- Format:

Slide 1:
[HOOK]

Slide 2:
[Point 1 - specific detail or example]

Slide 3:
[Point 2 - build on slide 2]

Slide 4:
[Point 3 - the key insight]

Slide 5:
[Proof or story that reinforces]

Slide 6:
[Counterintuitive twist]

Slide 7:
[Actionable takeaway]

Slide 8:
[CTA + question to drive replies]

---

TWEET:
[TURN THIS INTO A THREAD]
```

---

## Thread Formulas That Work

### The "Here's What Nobody Tells You" Thread
1. Hook: "Nobody talks about [X]"
2-5. Layer the insight from multiple angles
6. "Here's the [X] thing no one mentions"
7. CTA: "Reply with yours"

### The Step-by-Step Thread
1. Hook: "[Common goal] is simple. Not easy. But simple."
2-6. Numbered steps
7. "Do this for 30 days and [result]"
8. CTA

### The Story Thread
1. Hook: "I made [X] mistake. Cost me [Y]"
2. Setup: What happened
3. Realization: What you learned
4-6. The how/why unpacked
7. Actionable takeaway
8. CTA
