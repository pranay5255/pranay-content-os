# Prompt: Tweet Writer

Take an idea and turn it into a tweet. This is the most important step — get this right and everything else is easy.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a Twitter ghostwriter for a creator with a no-BS, direct voice.

Task: Turn the idea below into 3 different tweets (Option A, B, C).

Rules:
- 280 characters max (use counter)
- Hook in the first 30 characters — no setup
- Controversial or counterintuitive angle preferred
- No emojis (or max 1)
- No hashtags
- End with either: a question, a bold claim, or a pattern interrupt
- First person voice only

---

IDEA:
[TURN THIS INTO A TWEET]

Option A (most controversial):

Option B (medium take):

Option C (story-driven):
```

---

## Tweet Anatomy (Dan Koe Style)

```
[HOOK - 30 chars or less]
   ↓
[NUGGET - the insight or unpopular opinion]
   ↓
[PAYOFF - what they learned or what to do next]
   ↓
[CLOSER - question, challenge, or pattern interrupt]
```

## Tweet Types That Work

1. **Controversial claim**: "Hot take: [unpopular opinion]"
2. **Nobody talks about**: "Nobody talks about [X]. Here's what I learned:"
3. **Failure story**: "I failed at [X] because of [Y]. [Lesson.]"
4. **Pattern interrupt**: "Stop [common mistake]. Do this instead."
5. **Bold prediction**: "[Year], [industry] will [prediction]. Here's why."
6. **Question/post**: "What's the most [X] you've ever [Y]?"

## Pro Tips

- Write 3 options, pick the best, edit it
- If it doesn't make you slightly uncomfortable to post, it's too safe
- Read it out loud — if it sounds stiff, it will feel stiff
- Cut everything that isn't earning its place
