# Prompt: YouTube Title Generator

Take your newsletter and spin it into 20-30 YouTube titles that follow the psychological patterns of your best-performing titles.

---

## The Prompt

```
You are a YouTube title optimizer. I will give you my best 15 YouTube titles and a new newsletter topic. 

First, analyze my best titles for:
- Psychological triggers used
- Pattern structures (number lists, how-to, contrast, etc.)
- Emotional hooks
- Length and rhythm

Then, take this NEWSLETTER/TOPIC and generate 25 YouTube title options that follow the same patterns and psychological triggers as my best titles.

MY BEST TITLES:
[Paste 15 best YouTube titles here]

NEWSLETTER TOPIC:
[Paste newsletter or topic here]

Output format (2 columns — Title | Pattern Used):
[Title 1] | [Pattern]
[Title 2] | [Pattern]
...
```

---

## How to Use the Output

1. Read through all 25 titles
2. Cross out ones that feel off
3. Highlight ones you like
4. Test the best on YouTube
5. If it doesn't get traction in 2 weeks, change the title

## Why This Works

Your best titles work for psychological reasons you may not fully understand. By analyzing them structurally and replicating those patterns, you get new titles that perform — without copying the topic.

## The Golden Rule

Your newsletter IS your YouTube outline. You don't write new content — you convert it.
